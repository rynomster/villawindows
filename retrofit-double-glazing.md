<h1>Retrofit Double Glazing</h1>

<p>Retrofit double glazing is the process of replacing your existing single glass with new double glazed units (IGUs) into your original timber window frames.</p>

<h2>Why Choose Retrofit?</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Preserve the heritage character of your home.</li>
    <li>Significant reduction in heat loss.</li>
    <li>Reduced noise pollution.</li>
    <li>Eliminate condensation.</li>
</ul>

<h2>The Process</h2>
<p>Our skilled craftsmen carefully remove the existing glass and modify the timber frame to accommodate the thicker double glazed unit. We then seal and finish the window to ensure a perfect fit and long-lasting performance.</p>
