<h1>Casement Windows Retrofit Double Glazing</h1>

<p>Casement windows (those that open on hinges) are common in many New Zealand homes. We can retrofit double glazing into these frames just as effectively as we do with sash windows.</p>

<h2>The Benefits</h2>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Better Insulation:</strong> Stay warmer in winter and cooler in summer.</li>
    <li><strong>Reduced Condensation:</strong> Virtually eliminate weeping windows in the morning.</li>
    <li><strong>Noise Reduction:</strong> A quieter home environment.</li>
    <li><strong>Character Preservation:</strong> We use your existing timber frames, keeping the original aesthetic.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Our Approach</h2>
<p>We assess the condition of your casement frames and the strength of the hinges. Often, we upgrade the hinges to "friction stays" to support the additional weight of the double glazed units, ensuring your windows stay open at the desired angle.</p>
